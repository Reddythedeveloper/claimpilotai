# Case Resolution Note: CLM-88901
**Date:** 2025-11-12
**Issue:** Missing authorization denial for emergency appendectomy.
**Resolution:** 
We received a denial for missing prior authorization on an emergency procedure (47562). According to policy 102_4, emergency services do not require prior auth if admitted through the ED. 
I attached the ED triage notes and the operative report showing the emergent nature of the admission. The claim was resubmitted with these attachments and successfully paid on appeal.
**Takeaway:** Always append ED intake notes for emergent surgery denials to bypass the auth requirement.
